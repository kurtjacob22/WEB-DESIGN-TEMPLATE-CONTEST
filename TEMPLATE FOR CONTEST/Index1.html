PROGRAMS: / Technolympics
Students won top places in Different TLE contests
Technoskills

A GROUP of students won major awards in the 2017 TECHNOLYMPICS held Aug. 8-9 at Ramon Magsaysay Highschool

Gabriel Dacuan, of Lakan Dula Highshool, won first place in Web Design which covered topics about the theme which is "A Showcase of Marketable Products and Performances", but still it is a close fight with the contestant of Toress Highschool

Also, the contestant again of Lakan Dula Highschool, Mark Lao, won the first place in bookkeeping. Followed by the contestant of JDOM Highschool as 2nd placer, and followed by Ramon Magsaysay HighSchool as a 3rd placer.

On the Automotive section, the contestant of Osmeña Highschool won the 1st place followed by the contestant of Torress Highschool and Lakandula Highschool as 2nd and 3rd placer

In the Computer Hardware, The contestant of Lakandula Highschool won the first place respectively, while the Ramon Magsaysay Highschool got the second place in “Computer Hardware”,

The competition was the highlight of the 2017 Technolympics Celebration, an activity conducted by the TLE Teachers all-over the Philippines, which was participated by different schools, Private or Public. It was sponsored by Rodrigo Duterte, President of the Republic of the Philippines.The Web Design and “Computer Hardware” were administered by the best teachers in TLE.

All the activities were aimed to enhance the talents and skills of future Workers of the Philippines.The theme for this year’s activity was “A Showcase of Marketable Products and Performances” . With this, the TLE DEPARTMENT instills a culture of responsibility toward evolving trends in the profession that calls adaptability in challenges brought about by shifts in economy, laws, and regulations, and in society as a whole. More importantly, it propagates an environment of future leaders who will take on the role of leading the institution towards strengthening its relevance in the Livelihood profession.
